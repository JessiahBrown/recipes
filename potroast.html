Pot Roast

Pot roast is a hearty and flavorful dish made by slow-cooking a tough cut of beef with vegetables in a covered pot or slow cooker. The long, slow cooking process tenderizes the meat, infusing it with rich flavors from the cooking liquids and seasonings. This meal is easy to cook, versatile, cheap, and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • 3 pounds of boneless Beef Chuck
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Beef Broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker and sear the meat on all sides.
  2. Season the roast, then add the beef broth, worcestershire sauce, and onion. Then seal the lid.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and cook on full pressure for 30 minutes.
  4. Lower the pressure cooker, add the carrots and potatoes, and seal the lid.
  5. Return to full pressure and cook for 15 more minutes.
  6. Lastly Enjoy!
